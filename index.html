Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start learning vocabulary here?

Simply select a Lesson from the top menu. Each lesson contains a list of words. You can click the "info" icon to see the detailed meaning, synonyms, and example sentences for each word.

How does the "Sound" button work?

The sound button uses text-to-speech technology to help you hear the correct pronunciation of the word. It's a great way to improve your speaking and listening skills simultaneously.

What does the "Complete Learning" button do?

Once you feel confident about a word's meaning and usage, you can click "Complete Learning." This is designed to help you mentally track which words you've mastered in a session.

Are there different levels of difficulty?

Yes! Our lessons are organized by level numbers. Level 1 usually starts with basic everyday words, while higher levels introduce more complex and academic vocabulary.
